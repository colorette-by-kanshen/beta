ON A SCALE FROM -100 TO 100 Based on calculations made by the Contrast Calculator Engine
Contrast Check on Colorétte+ is a tool that lets you check the contrast of a color against both white and black. By entering a color code, you can see how it looks on white and black, and get a numeric value indicating the contrast level. The higher the contrast value is, the easier it is to see the color on darker backgrounds but harder on lighter backgrounds, and vice versa. This can help you choose colors that ensure your designs are accessible to all users.
